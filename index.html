Project Overview

This tool transforms how Gainwell analyzes PBM implementations by providing a structured, data-driven approach to capability comparison. By organizing product attributes across a hierarchical framework, the engine identifies concentration points for capability, intensity, and value delivery.

Current Stage: The engine is currently in alpha, prototyped against PBM NASPO and PBM MO data sources, with ongoing internal validation and refinement underway.

Key Capabilities

Scope Modeling: Hierarchical attribute organization for comprehensive product representation.
Data Aggregation: Rolls attribute-level insights up through product tiers for comparative analysis.
Implementation Comparison: Identifies capability gaps, intensity differences, and value concentrations across PBM products.
Extensibility: Designed to scale from prototype to production with minimal structural refactoring.

Roadmap Ahead

Near Term: Internal validation, weighting refinement, and SME review of scalers at the scope area level.
Mid Term: Sourcing Gainwell product data, automated data parsing, and testing infrastructure.
Future: GWT POC Box deployment and natural-language chat interface against the dataset.
